Help Page

Welcome to the help page! Here are instructions on how to use the search form:

Steps to Perform a Search:

  • Step 1: Select the section you want to search from the available options (e.g., Employment, Personal).
  • Step 2: Choose the publications you want to include in your search.
  • Step 3: Specify the date range for your search.
  • Step 4: Enter keywords related to the classified you're looking for.
  • Step 5: Click the Search button to simulate a search.

Other Options:

  • Help: Opens this help page for guidance.
  • Cancel: Clears the form and resets your selections.

If you have further questions, please contact our support team at support@classifieds.com.
